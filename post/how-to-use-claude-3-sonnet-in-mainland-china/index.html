Use Claude 3 Sonnet in Mainland China

Unfortunately, Claude, like ChatGPT, is not available in China.
The following will introduce how to overcome this limitation.

Prerequisites

  1. a proxy service or any other way to access the network outside the wall
  2. a Google account(recommended)

Steps

  1. use proxy service, choose one of the regions. I recommend the United States or the United Kingdom.
  2. visit the offical website of Claude
  3. register a Cluade account with a Google account
    Here you need to have an overseas mobile phone number to receive the verification code, You can find what you want here. By the way, choose the same region as the proxy address.
  4. then, you can use the Claude 3 Sonnet.
